# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to chad-ui.

## Current Policy

At this time, this project does **not accept issues or pull requests**.

## Discussion

Please use [**GitHub Discussions**](https://github.com/maximbelyayev/chad-ui/discussions) for all:
- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Questions
- General feedback

## Notes

This policy may change in the future as the project evolves.

Thank you for your understanding.
